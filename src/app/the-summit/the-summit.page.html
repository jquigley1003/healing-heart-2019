WHO I AM

Bring true curiosity and interest to...

your self....

To knowing — you.

To clarifying who you are — to yourself.

To naming who you know yourself to be.

To experiencing breath — breath breathing into your own being... And out.

To sitting in your own self and being willing to discover what naturally arises from within your being.

In the mission to grow in love with yourself and to be in solid loving relationship with yourself, you must know who you are.

Your strengths and weaknesses, your motivations and impasses, what juices your sense of aliveness and what suppresses your spirit, your sense of gender and sexuality, your creative expressions and your blocks, on and on — all to be discovered, explored, claimed and celebrated.
