Introduction to The Marrying Yourself Journey

The Marrying Yourself Journey is a year long online commitment to know yourself and embrace yourself more deeply than ever before.

As with other significant and primary relationships, the Journey begins with an invitation for openness and curiosity. It leads to greater understanding, authenticity and an experience of wholeness.

This Journey is about cultivating a foundation with yourself that can never be diminished by anyone or anything.

Different from other significant and primary relationships, there is no option for checking out. You are in this primary relationship for your whole life!

The Marrying Yourself Journey takes you through a thoroughly considered step-by-step, module by module, process.

It begins with anchoring into knowing yourself, and then leads you through the sacred ritual of getting engaged to yourself, re-working codependent patterns, empowering yourself through guided inner growth platforms, and concludes with the experience of self-marriage.

There are eighteen modules that include audio instruction, experiential guided exercises and assignments, Ted talks, performance art pieces, music and quotes to help you open to the richness of each springboard.

  1. The Welcoming, The Beginning
  2. Marrying Yourself Journey for the Masculine
  3. The Antidote for Codependency
  4. Who I Am
  5. Getting Engaged
  6. Commitment Mudra Flow
  7. Taming Your Mind, Embracing Your Heart, Loving Your Body, Nourishing Your Soul
  8. Self-Retrieval
  9. Embodying Goddess Wisdom
  10. Our Feminine and Masculine Selves
  11. The Four Archetypes of the Mature Masculine
  12. Romancing Yourself
  13. Parenting Yourself Well
  14. Boundaries and Empowerment
  15. Relationship With the Divine
  16. Intentions and Vows
  17. Wedding Time
  18. The Path of Self Marriage

The best way to approach this is to make it yours.

In addition to deepening intimacy with yourself, you will also be invited to share your Journey with other fellow travelers, creating a community in which we can get to know each other, share our experiences and insights, ask questions, and learn from one another. This community has the power to genuinely and meaningfully grow into being a great support to your engagement process and can ultimately witness and bless your union with yourself.

When you sign up, you will receive a zoom link that will give you this weekly opportunity for live interaction.

While the weekly zoom meeting provides a forum for processing your ongoing experience with others, this journey is all about you with you. It is about growing in beloved relationship with yourself, balancing your feminine and masculine, deepening in your commitment to self-care, boundaries, actualizing your passions and showing up for yourself.

The pace you choose to travel is up to you. Yet, there are instructions and guidelines for each module.

Why not consciously ground in yourself as your number one beloved relationship?
