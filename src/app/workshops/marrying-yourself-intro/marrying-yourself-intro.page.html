What Is The Marrying Yourself Journey?

The Marrying Yourself Journey is a year long online commitment to know yourself and embrace yourself more deeply than ever before.

As with other significant and primary relationships, the Journey begins with openness and curiosity, and leads to greater and greater understanding, authenticity and wholeness.

This Journey is about cultivating a foundation within yourself that can never be diminished by anyone or anything.

Different from other significant and primary relationships, there is no option for checking out. We are in this primary relationship with ourselves for our whole lives!

The Marrying Yourself Journey takes you through a carefully designed step-by-step, module by module, process.

It begins with anchoring into knowing yourself, and then leads you through the sacred ritual of getting engaged to yourself, re-working codependent patterns, and empowering yourself through guided inner work, and culminates with the experience of self-marriage.

MARRYING YOURSELF IS LIFE-CHANGING.

This is not just a course or a workshop. This is an inner journey that leads you to a sense of depth, substance, commitment, and a foundational shift with yourself that will never leave you and can not be diminished by anyone or anything.

The Path of Self Marriage

The Marrying Yourself Journey offers clear instructions and guidelines with each module, giving you the opportunity to go at your own pace. It also provides an interactive component with the option of participating in weekly zoom meetings, periodical gatherings and 1:1 personal consultations with me if you desire.

A sense of connection is a potent aspect of The Marrying Yourself Journey. You will be invited to share with other fellow travelers, creating a community in which we grow to know each other, share our experiences and insights, ask questions and learn from one another.

When you register for the journey, you will receive a zoom link for our weekly live connection. Also, if there are enough people in a geographic area, we can create periodical in-person gatherings. All of this can be explored in our weekly zoom gatherings

WHAT TO EXPECT
by the time you marry yourself:

An ARRIVAL WITH YOURSELF that is rooted in a profound knowingness and commitment to yourself as your number one relationship.

A sense of CONFIDENCE that you can and will RESOURCE YOURSELF rather than external sources.

A CLARITY with how to set PERSONAL BOUNDARIES based on SELF-ESTEEM AND SELF-RESPECT.

A visceral knowledge on how to BALANCE and INTEGRATE your FEMININE AND MASCULINE ENERGIES.

A TRUST IN YOURSELF that is unwavering based on taking vows with yourself, based on deep and thorough inner work.

An ability and desire to PRIORITIZE SELF-CARE and a sense of CREDIBILITY that you will do this for yourself, based on your experience of consistently showing up for yourself through the year's journey.

An ability to have HEALTHY RELATIONSHIPS WITH OTHERS grounded in the experience of retrieving yourself from unresolved past relationships, LISTENING TO YOURSELF AND LEARNING TO BE TRUE TO YOURSELF, FINDING YOUR VOICE TO SPEAK YOUR FEELINGS, NEEDS AND TRUTHS and to feel safe in your own self to live with an open heart.

Consistent trustworthy knowingness on how to POSITIVELY PARENT YOURSELF.

A willingness and commitment to practice SELF-COMPASSION and KINDNESS.

A SENSE OF SELF-RELIANCE AND EMPOWERMENT that you have what you need within yourself to BE AN AMAZING PARTNER TO YOURSELF and to ACTUALIZE your passions and dreams into realities.
