Introduction to The Marrying Yourself Journey

The Marrying Yourself Journey is a year long online commitment to know yourself and embrace yourself more deeply than ever before.

As with other significant and primary relationships, the Journey begins with openness and curiosity, and leads to greater and greater understanding, authenticity and wholeness.

This Journey is about cultivating a foundation within yourself that can never be diminished by anyone or anything.

Different from other significant and primary relationships, there is no option for checking out. We are in this primary relationship with ourselves for our whole lives!

The Marrying Yourself Journey takes you through a carefully designed step-by-step, module by module, process.

It begins with anchoring into knowing yourself, and then leads you through the sacred ritual of getting engaged to yourself, re-working codependent patterns, and empowering yourself through guided inner work, and culminates with the experience of self-marriage.

There are eighteen modules that include audio instruction, experiential guided exercises and assignments, Ted talks, performance art pieces, music and quotes to help you open to the richness of your own inner life.

The Path of Self Marriage

In addition to deepening intimacy with yourself, you will also be invited to share your Journey with other fellow travelers, creating a community in which we can get to know each other, share our experiences and insights, ask questions, and learn from one another. This community has the power to genuinely and meaningfully grow into being a great support to your engagement process and can ultimately witness and bless your union with yourself.

When you sign up, you will receive a zoom link to this weekly opportunity for live interaction.

While the weekly zoom meeting provides a forum for processing your ongoing experience with others, this journey is all about you with you. It is about growing in beloved relationship with yourself, balancing your feminine and masculine aspects, deepening in your commitment to self-care, setting and maintaining boundaries, actualizing your passions and generally showing up for yourself.

Each module has its own clear instructions and guidelines -- the pace you choose to travel is up to you.

Why not consciously ground yourself in your most primary beloved relationship?
