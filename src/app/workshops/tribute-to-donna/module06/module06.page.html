Assignment: Developing Attraction

DEVELOPING ATTRACTION

Duration: {{ audioDuration0603 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

1. Stand vertical — either outside, looking at a tree, or the sky or inside, looking in the mirror — and invoke: "I Invoke my Spiritual Self to be in the drivers seat as I embark on manifesting my male romantic companion now in my life. I invoke Her to be present and open to growing experiences of deepening in friendship and connection, enjoyment and pleasure. I invoke her so that my heart leads with openness and curiosity over judgement and resistance. I invoke her so that I can learn about emotional intimacy and meet ways that I can grow in vulnerability, connection and love. I open to learning about how romance can grow from a place of connection and enjoying someone. I invite myself to learn new ways of opening to love."

2. Learn more about how your friends chose their newer partners. What attracted them in the first place? How did they grow romantically interested? Talk directly about this with some friends.

3. Be open to dating again. Hold the posture that you are going to be in a romantic loving relationship and you are drawing this in. When you go out on a date, if you are enjoying your time with someone, play with two different channels. The first one is inspired by when we played dolls with our neighbor and I was your live doll but we made me blind. Remember? Lets for a few minutes, imagine when you are spending time with a nice guy, lets play that you are blind this time. (He he). Yes, you are blind and you can't see the person but you are able to experience his presence in all the other ways. What do you get about who he is if you can't see him? What is enjoyable about your time?

It's just kind of interesting, right? To use your senses other than visual.

Now, second channel — you put on a lens that only sees goodness and beauty. This lens magnifies what is nice appearing and good and beautiful about someone, inside and out, and this lens minimizes what is not appealing... so much so, that even for a few minutes of spiritual practice, what is not attractive is on the back burner or even off the burner. Just practice. This is a spiritual practice.

4. Call in your male match every day. Invoke and say good morning to him and talk to him and say good night to him. Remember when we were young and we would dream about who our husbands were and what they were doing that exact moment, knowing that they are in existence somewhere?

Well, let's do the same thing now. Let's wonder what he is up to and let's call him in. Talk to him. And visualize a shimmering ray of light connecting your soul star with his. Your soul star is about six inches above the crown of your head. You can also imagine intermingling pink and green cords connecting your heart chakra with his; an indigo blue cord connecting your minds and a red cord of light connecting your sexual centers. See these shimmering cords of connection alive, running energy in a circuit between the two of you.
