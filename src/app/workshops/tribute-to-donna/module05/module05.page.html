THE POWER OF MANIFESTING - SCRIPTING

We are going to proceed now into an exercise called Scripting. This tool is a very popular approach used for manifesting. Scripting is a manifestation technique that involves describing the experience you would like to manifest as if it has already happened. Scripting works for manifesting because it helps you cultivate the feeling of your desired experience. The more you feel something, genuinely feel it, the more you hold that vibration, the more you embody it and live it. There is a saying I love: "What you think, you feel. What you feel, you vibrate. What you vibrate, you attract. What you attract, you live."

You only need to script once per desire. It's not about quantity. It's about the quality. "The more you act in accordance with what you write, the faster you will be able to attract it into your life."

FIVE STEPS TO SCRIPTING

1. Be grateful for what you already have. Gratitude is one of the energies with the highest vibration according to the Emotional Guidance Scale. Write down three things you are genuinely grateful for — that you actually can feel the gratitude, not just mentally, but in your body and heart. Not things you know you should feel grateful for, but what you actually do feel grateful for.

2. Describe what you want to manifest. For the focus of this course, describe the romantic relationship you want to manifest. Write it in present tense. Write the description in detail, utilizing all five senses to help describe living this experience. The more specific you are, the easier it is to cultivate the feeling of what its like to experience your desire.

3. Clarify how it makes you feel. Write about your desire and how it makes you feel as if you have already manifested it. Yes, actually feel into the experience of being with a man who helps you to feel beautiful, who helps you to feel a sense of being supported, known, seen and loved. Just try it on, or more exactly, just feel it on! Think of how you felt this before in your life, perhaps earlier years with Michael. Remember what it feels like. Or, call upon movies that demonstrate this feeling to you and you recognize it. You know it. You know it inside of you. This opens the doorway to feeling into it. This is a very important part because according to the law of vibration — everything is made up of energy that vibrates at a specific frequency. The Law of Attraction is that similar vibrations are drawn together. So the key to manifesting what you want is to identify the feeling and align with it by embodying it. So, write about it and step into it as if it is truly now happening. And let it feel real good. Remember, goose, you know how to imagine. You know how to see magic. Use your skills at imagining magic for this exercise.

4. Be open to other possibilities. It is said that the biggest mistakes in scripting is getting too attached to the outcome you desire and then using it to prove your negative belief system. Let the Universe know that you are open to experiencing this or something better than you are imagining. The affirmation to use is: "I surrender my timelines, agendas, and plans to the care of the Universe. I trust that everything is working out for the highest good of all. I know I will manifest this or something better."

5. Thank the Universe. The final step to scripting is to thank the universe for this opportunity to co-create your reality. "I am so grateful for this opportunity to co-create my desires with you. I am open to receiving universal guidance, spiritual solutions and creative ideas. I step back and follow your lead." Closing with gratitude raises your vibration even more and aligns you with even greater mojo juice.

Remember, you are playing.

And, you are learning something about spiritual principle and living in the feeling currently of what you want to manifest in the future.

Read over your script and let the words elevate your vibration.

Remember, why are we doing this?

To expand your state of consciousness to include more magic in your life. Coz why not?

Play with the spiritual vibrations, vs. getting attached to outcomes. This is open-ended, this is the Goddess's timeline....
