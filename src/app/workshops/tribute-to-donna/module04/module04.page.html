FORGIVENESS AND HEALING

This module is a bit of a deep dive. Enter into it when you have energy and time to give to it. Allow enough time to go through all of the steps without interruption. Perhaps a few hours.

Okay, here we go. Here we grow. Here we heal.

Assignment: Self-Forgiveness

SELF-FORGIVENESS ASSIGNMENT

Duration: {{ audioDuration0402 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

1. Write down what you did that you think you are being punished for. Be specific. Don't hold back. Don't be vague. Get it out on paper. No one will ever see this but you. It is between you and your soul and spirit. Write it all. Breathe, let yourself feel, be with it....

2. Now, I want you to remember everything that was inside of you as you did these "wrongs" — I want you to write about everything that was going on inside of you. What she was carrying from the past, what she was feeling, why was she so shut down. This is a doorway into compassion and empathy as we fill in the details of what was going on.

3. Now, rewrite and integrate the first step and second step but this time I want you to replace the “You” that you hold as guilty with either me or Nicole. (I know you access tons of compassion and empathy for either one of us. So, use which ever one of us that will help to unlock your heart right now.) So, for example, if you had written in the first step, "I didn't talk to him at the fireworks" and in number two step, you wrote, "I was feeling so upset and overwhelmed, like I just couldn't do this again, it was so disappointing because I had a little hope and now I felt like I was falling into a hole."

Now, in this third step, replace the "I" with another person who you empathize easily with, lets say me, and you write: "Deva didn't talk to him at the fireworks. She just couldn't even look his way. Her heart was in her feet. She was so disappointed and scared and overwhelmed that this was happening again. A nightmare." And write the story all over again, this time using and filling in the details of the story using me or Nicole instead of you.

4. Now, take a breather. Go for a walk but stay with yourself. Be with your heart. Be with forgiveness. Your willingness to bring forgiveness to me or Nicole in that situation. Your opening to willingness to fully forgive yourself as you would me or Nicole and how you would help us to forgive ourselves. With your very practical wisdom approach.

Don't scatter your energy. This is a sacred walk. Walk to Poppy's Point. Breathe.

5. Upon your return, write yourself a forgiveness note. As you would write me or Nicole, with the same practical wisdom approach you bring to either of us.

6. Now, as corny as it sounds, go to a part of your house that feels sacred. Maybe in front of the new art piece you bought yourself of the spiritual woman in your bedroom or the horse in your foyer. Get on your knees. Get a blanket to kneel on first. Be kind. And bring your head to the ground. In child's pose. You are a child, my love, in the Universe.

Ask for full forgiveness. Ask for full release. Open to trusting that all of what happened is so much bigger than you. You were just a player in the big drama. Ask for total healing.

And while you are there, also ask for your heart to fully forgive Michael for leaving you. As you are on your knees, surrendering, we know that we don't know. There is a Mystery to all of it. We don't know why he couldn't reach for any other option than what he did. We don't know why it was his destiny. We do know it is so much bigger than what you did or didn't do. Breathe, stay there as long as you need to. This is between you and Spirit. Don't cut it short for some superficial reason. You are here, in the fire of the soul — stay until you know its time to rise up.

7. Rise up and go immediately to the mirror. Look into your sincere eyes. Bring your palms of your hands to your cheeks. Touch yourself with the hands of compassion, as Mom would bring to you, as your spirit guides would bring to you. Be their hands. Touch and be tender. This is what you need. Utter tenderness and forgiveness, healing and love.
