WHO YOU ARE AT 70

As you greet yourself at 70, you are not the same as ever before. Actually, you have transformed tremendously in your sixties.... Almost as much as you did your first five years of your life! You journeyed through tragic trauma that forever reverberates, you picked up the pieces and put them back together, differently; you became self-sufficient in ways you never thought you would be; and you re-created your self, your life, and your home!!!! You live in your own energy field, just like your home reflects one hundred percent you and only you, your days are reflections of your spirit and how you dance as you on this planet.

Assignment: Self-Inquiry Journaling

1. How have you changed since 2014?

2. How are you different than you have ever been before?

3. How are you the same as you always have been in your life?

4. What do you like most about yourself? Be thorough.

5. How are you proud of yourself? List the ways!

6. What qualities do you most value about yourself in relation to others?

7. Write a short poem that describes how you experience your own spirit.

8. Write a love letter to yourself.
