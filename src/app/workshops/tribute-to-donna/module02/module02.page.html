WHO YOU ARE AT 70

WEED OUT THE BUTS

Duration: {{ audioDuration0101 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

This YES has to include your true willingness. Your willingness to grow. Your willingness to further heal. Your willingness to expand. Your willingness to grow close with another man in your life.

It has been said that our thoughts shape our realities. Often it is the unconscious thoughts that have the pull. Like an undertow. We can think positive thoughts but if we feel pessimistic, the pessimism will rule. It is HOW we feel about something that comes into fruition more than even the thoughts.

Let's give you an opportunity to be right where you are and include the buts!

Assignment: Clearing Out The Buts

MAKE A LIST OF ALL THE BUTS

Duration: {{ audioDuration0102 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

1. Make a list of all the buts that you can think of. Be thorough. For example, these are some things I have heard you say: "I don't think it's meant to be in my life."

2. When you are done with this, take a look at them. Consider loosening up identifying with these thoughts. This time, instead of believing them as true, bring curiosity to them. See them as stories that attempt to explain why things are the way they are. This time bring a fresh look at these thoughts as blueprints for how life takes shape. Thoughts have power. These thoughts on your list do not help and instead, block the flow that propels future possibilities.

Even if you deep down inside yourself believe they are true — proven to be true by the Universe — would you be willing to step out of that box of thinking and "try on" living without them? Can you envision not investing in these thoughts as true, even when they arise?

These thoughts are only useful for one thing — to protect you from not expecting anything. And if you don't expect anything, then you will be less disappointed.

What if we develop another way to protect you? Here are some options:

3. Learn to witness and observe what is happening without making up a negative spin narrative about it.

4. Practice not personalizing any of it.

5. Hold steady to your openness, no matter what the dating scene looks like.

6. Remember that stepping out of the old automatic ways of protecting yourself into a refreshing approach, you can access a more wholehearted YES to embarking on this course.

And you know me, I think in terms of the bigger picture.

7. Soul level vision. I want to offer a different vision to anchor into: Ground in the understanding that there will be love in your life again, whether it is in this life or another time.

Your soul will absolutely experience romantic love again.

Don't you dare cast doubt on it.
Remember, we are releasing doubt and honoring the power of thought and the feeling underlying thought.

8. If this thought is not lifting you or generating positive vibes, then go back to the list of pouring out negativity. It would mean you're just not done with it yet. Go through this exercise over and over until you feel lifted and open and can truly say, "I will love again" and mean it! By meaning it, you can embark on this course with a wholehearted "Yes!"

9. When you have gotten to your wholehearted YES, let's now do something with these old thoughts that you don't need anymore.

Want to tear them out of your journal and rip them up? Burn them? Cross them out? Color over them with pretty color markers and crayons. Do something to release and transform these old thoughts that are no longer necessary.

10. Walk around for the next two days with the thought coursing through your body, "One day I will love again!" Remember, on a soul level, this is not just a possibility or probability, it is an Absolute!

Okay, so now you are ready.

You are embarking on this course of transformation with a big YES without a but!
