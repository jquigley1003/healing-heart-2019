WHO YOU ARE AT 70

As you greet yourself at 70, you are not the same as ever before. Actually, you have transformed tremendously in your sixties.... Almost as much as you did your first five years of your life! You journeyed through tragic trauma that forever reverberates, you picked up the pieces and put them back together, differently; you became self-sufficient in ways you never thought you would be; and you re-created your self, your life, and your home!!!! You live in your own energy field, just like your home reflects one hundred percent you and only you, your days are reflections of your spirit and how you dance as you on this planet.

Assignment: Self-Inquiry Journaling

1. How have you changed since 2014?

2. How are you different than you have ever been before?

3. How are you the same as you always have been in your life?

4. What do you like most about yourself? Be thorough.

5. How are you proud of yourself? List the ways!

6. What qualities do you most value about yourself in relation to others?

7. Write a short poem that describes how you experience your own spirit.

8. Write a love letter to yourself.

CLARIFY WHAT YOU WANT

What are the gifts of a seasoned man? Just like us seasoned women, most men know themselves by this point. And some men have cultivated great depth, gratitude, and heart by their older years that they did not have access to in earlier years.

So, if we were at a dating game, our own version and you were to choose from these qualities for male company, what would you choose:

  1. A Good Head of Hair
  2. An Athletic Body
  3. Financial Abundance
  4. A Great Heart
  5. Intelligent
  6. Progressive
  7. A Great Listener
  8. Funny
  9. Lives a Fulfilled Life
  10. Values Family and Friends
  11. Good looking
  12. Interested in You and Able to Help You Grow
  13. Has a Back Bone and Simultaneously Can Own Things and Apologize When Need Be.

Assignment: Qualities For Male Company

1. You can pick four. What four are most important to you? Write down these qualities.

Okay, okay, I must say, I just wrote down a pretty good list. We would love all of these qualities in a guy for you but not even Michael met all of these qualities. But what the hell, add to the list! What else needs to be on this list that would meet your heart's desire.

2. Now, number the qualities in order of importance to you.

3. Now star the ones that are deal breakers, absolute MUSTS for you.

Caution: Do NOT fall into despair here and say this does not exist. Already done that. It lowers your energy. It shrinks your capacity to dream.

Remember, you will love again, on a soul level, absolutely!

COMMUNE WITH MICHAEL

4. Now, as you dream into what you want in male company/partnership today, write a letter to Michael. Yes, the man you spent all of your adult life with.

Don't you think, on some unconscious level, your relationship with him may be impacting you and your true availability? Well, I do.

So, write him and tell him who he is to you and who he will always be. Tell him about your desire to be fulfilled and love again. Tell him how nobody replaces him. Tell him what you think is so obvious that you never have to say it. Say it! Tell him! Ask him for his blessing.

5. Then, on a separate paper, ask him for his love, support and blessing to love some more with someone else... Then, close your eyes and talk to his spirit and ask him for his blessing. Then, burn this paper and take it to a special tree near Poppy's Point and offer it into the earth of this tree. Then bring your hands to the tree and breathe and receive. Open to feel yourself being released and blessed.
