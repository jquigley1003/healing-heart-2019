Current Workshops

Transformational Integrative Group Psychotherapy Training for Psychotherapists ~~ A seven month training, meeting once a month, in the dynamics of experiential therapy -- from the baseline of the conceptual framework for experiential work, exposure to the work in the client role, and then eventually learning how to facilitate the work as a therapist. CEU credits are offered to LCSW, LPC, MSW.


Marry Yourself Journey ~~ Marry Yourself Journey meets once every 40 days, over an eight month period of time. It is a commitment process in growing to know and love yourself, release what doesn't serve you, learn to set boundaries, claim what you really want, balance feminine and masculine energy, and live into passionate fulfillment with yourself. This process will lead to taking vows with yourself in a wedding ceremony -- a marriage that holds no possiblity for divorce. This journey is about cultivating a sound and unfaltering commitment to yourself that can not be taken away by anyone or anything. This gathering begins on June 5th, 2016 (2.5 hours each time of meeting). If you are interested, immediately contact me.


Tribal Time ~~ A year commitment to monthly gatherings centered around personal growth themes, using ritual, movement, meditation, yoga, dance, and experiential exploration. Yearly themes of the past have included: Sacred Embodiment, Endless love; Surprise: Embodying the Higher Frequency; Loving Into This.


Self-Parenting Trainings ~~ Course work designed to train in positive parent functioning and have opportunities to imprint the development of healthy ways to respond to oneself on a foundational level.


Conscious Grieving and The Power of Love ~~ This workshop offers guidance and support for the bereavement process, rituals for honoring loss as well as ways to discover how love never dies and ways to stay connected to loved ones who have departed.


Nourishing Your Love: Basic Training in Spiritual Partnership ~~ Co-led with my husband of 32 years, we have distilled a tremendous amount of informations and tools to what we believe really makes a difference. This is an intensive training that is aimed at teaching skills to clear out the pipes of built up resentments and hurts and to offer communication tools for healthy conflict resolution and developing positive regard for one another. We introduce the skill or method, demonstrate it using our own material from our relationship, and give you time to practice each skill together with your partner.


Laughter Yoga ~~ This workshop is based on body-mind exercises that create powerful positive changes in your body, mind and emotions. This is a fun new exercise system sweeping the world, developed by Dr. Kataria, an Indian medical doctor who studied the positive healing effects of laughter. You don't need a reason to laugh or even a good sense of humor. Nor do you need to feel happy to engage in laughter therapy. It has been scientifically proven that the body does not know the difference between real laughter and artificial laughter and thus, receives beneficial physical healing from both. Laughter yoga cultivates playfulness and joy, lightening up, and physiological positive healing changes.
