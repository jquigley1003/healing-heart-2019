THE SPIRITUAL ADULT, DHARMA, AND VERTICAL ALIGNMENT

Duration: {{ audioDuration1502 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

Practicing Vertical Alignment is a way of stepping into a posture that organically helps you to embody spiritual connection and antidote codependent patterns.

Let's try it on. Stand... arms above, feet shoulder length apart, clasp hands press up to skies, press feet into earth, now separate hands, shoulder length apart, palms facing inward, feeling the energy from above, coming down through your body, into the earth.. As a tree, your arms extend up, like tree branches; your torso, steady like the trunk of the tree; the soles of your feet rooting into the earth, down deep into the earth... You are a channel of energy from above, through you, and below you, into the earth. A portal of energy of light, a vertical portal.

Be aware of your back bone, be aware of your feet, your Hara center, energy pouring into you from above, through you, below you. You are connected, you are aligned. You are vertical.

Now, slowly lower your arms but still feel as if they are above. Staying vertical.

Vertical Alignment is a opening to experiencing your relationship to above and below, sky and earth. The higher energies, manifesting as you, grounding into the earth. Vertical Alignment has the ability to open you to experiencing yourself as divine manifestation.

Vertical is a doorway into a spiritual shift. It is a way to step into connection with spirit manifested as form. Sacred Embodiment.
