A SHARING FROM A MAN IN THE MARRYING YOURSELF JOURNEY
There is a duality in this experience that enables us to collectively feel supported and be vulnerable. This allows us to approach and face our personal experiences in ways that I don't think we otherwise could have. Deeper than at least I've ever done in my 31 years of being and existing, knowing that I am sharing it with 10+ other men. Not suffocated by shame and left to journey into atonement, we are able to take bigger breaths and deeper dives to understand the full scope of our being, then our interaction and experiencing of the rest of existence, then perceptions of those realities. We feel connected to ourself through this process because it is just to that...a commitment to always be the connection... a feeling of connection to the source regardless of the quality of our experiences, regardless of the outcome. We are vulnerable in our courage, it is courageous to be vulnerable. I am my beloved, my beloved is me!

I love myself and therefore I love each of you men and the bond we have formed!
