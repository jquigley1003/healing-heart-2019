INTRODUCTION TO ROMANCING YOURSELF

Romancing yourself is seductive, honoring, catering, engaging, sensual, titillating, sexy. Romancing yourself is to be in the Wow with yourself. Wooing yourself is the experience of how amazing you are. To claim it. To celebrate with how exquisite and dimensional you are.

Romancing yourself is a different flavor of self-love than anything else.

Romancing yourself is an experience of being enthralled with your-self. Not in a conceited way, but rather, an in love kind of way! It is about being in the wow of yourself. It is being fascinated and entertained and loving and honoring and grateful to be in the company of yourself. Just like when you are in love with someone else. But here, you are growing in love with you! It is a feeling state. When we are in love, when we are being courted, when we are being seen as beautiful and wanted, we feel more beautiful. Our biochemistry shifts when we feel in love. Endorphins flow. Serotonin, oxytocin, and dopamine flow. We feel good.

To Romance... yourself.... To flirt, to court, to woo... who? You!!!
