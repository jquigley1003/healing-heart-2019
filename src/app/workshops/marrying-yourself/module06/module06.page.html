TAMING YOUR MIND

Duration: {{ audioDuration602 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

Bottom line, for a loving relationship with yourself, your mind must be an ally, not a foe.

Beginning with the awareness that you are in relationship with your mind, you can then have the awareness of the choice you have on where you want to focus your mind. This leads into the essential commitment to tame your mind — to not believe everything it says, to soothe it with sounds and mantra, to learn how to be a gentle yet firm positive parent with it.

HOW TO TAME YOUR MIND

Duration: {{ audioDuration603 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

Remember, you are in relationship with your mind.

So, the first step is to notice what your mind is up to — what it is chewing on.

Second step, ask yourself, “Is what my mind focusing on right now serving me?

If the answer is “Yes, it is important for me to problem-solve this issue,” then we want to be most productive with this as we can.

One way to do this is with mind-mapping.

BE THE ALPHA WITH YOUR MIND

Duration: {{ audioDuration604 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

When the response is “No, what my mind is focusing on right now is not serving me” then it is important to shift the mind's focus. Have you heard of the term “Alpha” — the “Alpha” wolf in a pack is the dominant one and the one who helps to keep everyone else in their right place so that the pack is strong and can survive. What if you became the alpha with your own mind?

So lets review. You are witnessing your mind and naming what it is doing. You are asking if it is serving you. If the answer is no, you are stepping in and re-directing your mind. You can't help what you mind initially thinks. Thoughts float in and out. But you can choose to either fuel these thoughts or not.

When your mind is actively thinking thoughts you don't want, experiment with making the sound SHHHHHHHHHH... this is the archetypal sound mothers make — and fathers too — to their babies all over the world — because... it is soothing...

Another great tool for taming the mind is the mantra. Mantra, sound vibration...

I am going to include the power of mumbo jumbo and energy medicine exercises of the zip up, cross lateral and shaking and the crown pull to help you tame your mind. When you change your physiology, you change your emotional state.

By learning to be the alpha with your own mind, you are intentionally taming your mind.

Being in right relationship with your mind is one of the most important aspects in being engaged to yourself.

To have a loving and gentle relationship with your mind is to have a loving and gentle relationship with your self.
