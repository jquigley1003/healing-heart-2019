YOUR WEDDING CEREMONY

You are standing or sitting before the beautiful alter you created for yourself.

Breathe. Take your time.
Look deep into your eyes.
Honor yourself.
Ritualize your wedding.
Maybe you light a candle to represent the love you are committed to with yourself.
Maybe you have bowls of water near the alter and you wash your hands and feet to symbolize cleansing of the past and new beginning.
Maybe you invoke Vertical Alignment.
Maybe you smudge yourself or ask someone else to smudge you.
Whatever it is, make it yours, make it holy.
Then speak your vows and commitments as you make eye contact with yourself.
Consciously give — and — receive each vow, commitment and intention.
Offer yourself the wedding ring or symbol of your marriage.
Receive the wedding ring or symbol of marriage.
Breathe. Take your time. Truly, no rush.
This is not a performance piece.
This is you authentically, genuinely, showing up for your self.
Arriving, with yoursel, to yourself, in a most powerful way.
You, here now, pledging and demonstrating your commitment to yourself.
Your self-marriage will exist inside of you for the rest of your life.
Remember, no one and nothing can ever diminish your marriage to yourself.
Be bold, be brave, be all of who you are as you marry all of who you are.

After you make your vows and receive the marriage symbol, pause, breathe. don't rush. See into your eyes and then as you take leave of the alter, allow yourself to be seen by others. Receive hugs and being celebrated and witnessed in one of the most intimate experiences of your life.
Or, if you did not invite people to your wedding ritual, turn to nature. Be seen by The Wonder.
