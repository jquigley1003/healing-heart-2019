Welcome, welcome, welcome as you embark on this wonderful, transformative experience with yourself.

This journey begins with getting engaged to yourself. This commitment is the alignment of your heart, mind and soul that has arrived at this time to say, “Yes, I am committed to growing to be able to truly know and claim that I am worthy of my love. I am worthy of the best inside of me — I am worthy of receiving the love that I usually bring to others. I now choose to direct this love to myself. I commit to loving myself as the number one beloved relationship in my life.”

The Marrying Yourself Journey is truly a sacred journey that you are taking with yourself.

Take a deep breath and pause long enough to truly honor and thank yourself for all the inner work and growth you have already experienced that allows for you to arrive ~~ here ~~ embarking on this marrying yourself journey.

“Yes, I want to marry myself!” (Try on these words or something like them! Try on saying them out loud.)
How does it feel? How do you feel as you say this? And, do you also allow yourself to receive it?

Can you amplify these words with your aliveness so that your intention to be in the most loving relationship with yourself as possible bears fruits for you right now, in this very moment, as you hit the gas pedal and bring your heart juice to, “Yes, I want to marry myself!”
(Go for it, give yourself to loving…. Yourself!)

Commitment to Mudra Flow

Duration: {{ audioDuration06 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

Commitment because this is a commitment. A Commitment that nurtures your core, your body, your heart, your mind.

Mudra because you are participating in a non-verbal flow of expression of hand gestures that invoke an inner posture, a positive vibration and expanded state of consciousnesses.

Flow because as you engage in the Commitment Mudra Flow, you are allowing flow. You are participating in flow. You are letting yourself shift and commune deeper with yourself in the process of flow.

Practice it daily. Enter into it as a sacred way of communing with yourself. A spiritual practice. An invocation.

Steep in it and allow it to re-arrange you.

Welcoming Practices

Duration: {{ audioDuration07 * 1000 | date:'mm:ss' }}

~~ Make sure you have a journal that you love dedicated completely to your Marrying Yourself Journey.

~~ Write love notes (or letters) to yourself every day, even just a sentence. And that one sentence may lead to more....Go with the flow and address yourself with a message whether you feel like it or not. A loving relationship does not depend on how you feel but rather the “spiritual practice" of showing up with love, no matter what you are feeling.

~~ Practice the Commitment Mudra Flow daily. Experiment and practice it in front of a mirror, in nature, when you wake up, before bed, when you feel low, when you feel good, before you spend time with someone else, (especially someone you feel romantic with.) Make it part of your daily routine, as you do with brushing your teeth.
