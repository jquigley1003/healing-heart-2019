LOVER - SHARINGS FROM MEN...

The "Lover Archetype" feels connected to the Universe, which I experience as the “Great Mystery”. I often experience the lover archetype through my senses, which are heightened and feel awake. This is both a blessing and it can also be a burden — if not tamed with boundaries.

The lover can easily be seduced, if not grounded in the KING. The boundaries from the King are critically important to how I am able to manage all that I feel — versus being chaotic and boundless with my feelings and senses.

As the lover, I have learned how supremely impactful it is for me to be clear and committed to my boundaries, in alignment with the King archetype.

If not guided by The King archetype, "I" the Lover, will chase the intensity of all that I feel and sense and then be left with wanting more. I will therefore find myself creating messy situations, with lots of drama because I'm looking for the lover in me to be met outside of myself (which is insatiable).

As I mature and season as a man, I experience my lover archetype self loving my-self unconditionally and therefore, grounding in my primary relationship with myself as first and foremost.

I validate my lover by validating my authenticity.

The lover seeks connection and reciprocation. He may or may not get that reciprocation from others so therefore he must not depend on those outside of himself to validate his lover self as ONLY he can.

Feeling and sensing can lead to hurt and pain but NOT numbness. Better to feel than to be numb. Even in the hurt and pain, I find myself alive and honoring of the “Great Mystery,” which I experience as within everyone and everything.

I also recognize that I must be kind to myself and love myself along the way, all the while being kind and loving to all others. A Win Win! This is The Way of The Lover archetype as I live it.

-Jeffree S. Gars
LCSW Psychotherapist/Counselor, DC Doctor of Chiropractic

MAGICIAN - SHARINGS FROM MEN...
“I want to know if you can disappoint another to be true to yourself. If you can bear the accusation of betrayal and not betray your own soul. If you can be faithless and therefore trustworthy.”
Oriah Mountain Dreamer The Invitation

I am a magician.

On many different levels.

I'll show you the most obvious demonstration by a magic trick.

By careful manipulation, deception, misdirection and story - I entertain you.

It sets your brain whirling - how?

And we don't tell you. We want you to enjoy and to struggle with how and why.

In my life - I have been a magician, a photographer, a priest, a psychotherapist, a father.

I attempted social change and failed - I have been a 'wrench in the gears' - subversive, a skeptic, a counter cultural irritant, a political activist and at present - I am a change agent.

As a part of those things - necessary to the doing of those things - I have also been a thief, a liar, a manipulator, an opportunist, an adulterer...fueling my shadow - and its impact... my self/ego destruction, relationship abuse, and voluntary defrocking.

---------------------------
The magician watches and thinks. (observing ego)

He observes and makes sense of (too much) information. (knowing what others don't)

He helps people see and make decisions. (manipulation)
---------------------------

I was a lonely child - a latchkey kid - alone. I didn't know at the time - I was an abused child.

This forged an introversion. I wanted to be liked. I was sweet and funny. I was and am mischievous. I love girls and animals.

I was and am an observer - of what was around me.

I saw racism. hypocrisy. injustice. exclusion.

I don't like people.

And I saw inclusion, art, helpers, love and embrace.

I love people.

I see the darkness of the gothic south, the lies of the church and white people, the manipulation in politics for personal gain.

My generation (the boomers) wanted to be different. We all saw what was. We intended to get rid of the "man" and replace it with love, peace and rock and roll (and drugs). We sold out.

I see how people can be brought into the sacred - in nature, a pew, a therapist couch, a photograph - in an embrace.

At my best - I saved and restored life, found lost souls, included the excluded, saw beauty and loved big.

At my worst - I was not aware of the 'same time' destruction my shadow wrought.

I am at present - humbled, quiet, and seeking to restructure how I am to use my power for good. To control my grandiosity and keep my ego deconstructed. I want to recover and restore. I want to love big.

Be a magician.

Observe, know, manipulate for good.

Know your shadow.

--Thomas Watson Query
June 19, 2022
