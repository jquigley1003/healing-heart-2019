Re-arranged, Never The Same: The Nature of Grief

An extensive overview of themes that arise when grieving, how to navigate through grief and how to explore ways to staying in "current relationship" with the loved one who has departed.

Toolbox of Hope: For When Your Body Doesn't Work

An interactive workbook for people living with illness and disability. It is the book I would have liked as a child when I was diagnosed with Rheumatoid Arthritis. Its purpose is to help children and their families deal with the changes and feelings that occur when living with disease. Adults also benefit from this experiential approach to making friends with one's illness.
