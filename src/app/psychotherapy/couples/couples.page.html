Couples Therapy

Couples Therapy offers a safe container and guidance for clearing out the “gunk” that is in the way of feeling the connection between partners. In a sense, couples therapy is emotional plumbing for the coupleship as a whole and both partners individually. Relationships, by nature, are designed to bring up what is unresolved in people’s psyches and from childhood. Beloved relationships begin with enough chemistry to jumpstart the process of clearing out old pain and then it inevitably becomes essential to learn new ways of relating to keep the flow alive. Approached in a conscious way, with guidance and new tools, relationships are opportunities for healing one's self and each other. I like to say that the primary beloved relationship is not a neutral force; at some point, it usually becomes sandpaper to the original wound and rips off the scab — and then, with guidance, it can become the most effective medicine that helps to bring true healing. Struggle and conflict are not the issue — these are inevitable in relationship. The key is how a couple learns to approach the conflicts and how to repair and bridge so that both people feel held by the other with respect and a sense of positive regard. I welcome all couples: LGBT, fluid, polyamorous, heterosexual, monogamous, etc.
