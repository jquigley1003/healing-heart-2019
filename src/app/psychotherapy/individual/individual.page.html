Individual Therapy

Individual Therapy is a collaborative, intentional relationship committed to unfolding courage, self-honesty, authenticity, self-compassion and conviction to align one’s behaviors and interactions with what is uncovered as one’s true self. In the process of psychotherapy, it becomes apparent that the causes of our suffering are not rooted in external circumstances, but rather in how we respond to them. It is the same for our conditioning, beliefs and feelings that arise within us. It is not a matter of what we think or feel, but rather how we respond to ourselves. Therapy offers an expansion of your toolbox so that you can respond with more conscious compassion, discipline, and love. Within the context of the psychotherapy relationship, a “corrective” experience is offered, a “re-wiring” naturally occurs and your ways of attending to yourself evolve — as well as your other relationships. We are a team dedicate to shifting your internal map so that you grow to be thoroughly comfortable in your own skin and live in alignment with your authentic self.
