Life Coaching

Healing Heart Coaching provides an integrative coaching approach that is dedicated to enhancing your quality of life by helping you to move forward toward actualizing your visions into realities.

It is an interactive, engaging process that opens you to the field of possibilities, helps you to clarify your objectives, upgrade your relationships with yourself and others, manifest what you really think, feel and want and develop your skill set to manifest your personal and professional goals.

Healing Heart Coaching sheds light on how to unplug from dysfunctional, self-sabotaging patterns of behavior – and how to replace them with functional, proactive tools, grounded in self-esteem, self-respect and self-love.

In Healing Heart Coaching, we construct a plan for you to work with between sessions, utilizing exercises and behavior contracts to enhance your sense of well-being and help you to meet your goals. My intention is to help you to expand your tool box to ultimately experience your sense of wholeness, which I see as your birthright. Some of the tools you may learn include mindfulness practices, “energy medicine” tools, body awareness and movement, communication skills, relationship training, resiliency training, the Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT), and concrete practical actions that help you to manifest your desires.
