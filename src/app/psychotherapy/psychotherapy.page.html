Psychotherapy

I see Psychotherapy as a path of healing and transformation that is founded in a courageous commitment to self-exploration -- within the safe container of a therapeutic relationship that is based on equality and mutual respect.

Psychotherapy offers guidance to free ourselves from old pain and trauma, and helps to release old beliefs, defenses and other behavioral patterns that once helped us to survive -- but now no longer serve us. It also offers corrective emotional experiences that shift the internal "emotional blueprint," developing alternative templates for more authentic and satisfying relationships.

This occurs through various ways, to mention a few --- the therapeutic relationship itself, training in developing and internalizing positive parenting, mindful experience and changing thinking and behavioral patterns.
