About Me

Because I love being a psychotherapist, I naturally offer a welcoming spirit to each person I sit with. I bring interest and inquiry to your present day life situations, your relationships, and the transformations you are longing to bring about.

When I agree to work with someone, I enter into a deep commitment to you and and to our relationship that is designed to serve as a vehicle for healing. As a seasoned psychotherapist, I have experience in a wide variety of issues and life-situations. Although I believe the most important aspect in a therapeutic relationship is the safety and sense of connection between therapist and client, I also bring my own “personal toolbox” that has grown rather extensive through the years, including experiential approaches such as Psychomotor Therapy, Transactional analysis, Trauma Resiliency Method, Emotional Freedom Technique, EMDR and Brain Spotting, Mindfulness practice, Energy medicine work, Movement therapy, etc. I facilitate many workshops and trainings. Some of these are Transformational Integrative Experiential Group Psychotherapy Institute: The Nature of The Grieving Process; How to Deepen through Illness; Marrying Yourself Journey: Tribal Time; Council for All Beings, Reverence for all of Life; The Power of Sacred Rituals; Inner Parent Training; Embracing Your Shadow; Goddess Archetypes; The Art of Enjoyment; and Celebration of Movement.
